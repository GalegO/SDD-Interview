# Discovery 5W1H (02.01_5w1h_discovery.md)

> [!TIP]
> The 5W1H method helps to understand the project in its most practical dimensions, defining the proposition with clarity.

## 1. What? (O Quê)
> What exactly will be delivered in the end? (Product, service, scope, etc.)
- A structured collection of Markdown (`.md`) documents, containing the details of all information provided by the user.
- The scope and richness of this final documentation will be directly proportional to the depth of the details mapped during the interview.

## 2. Who? (Quem)
> Who are the direct and indirect actors in this process? (Users, stakeholders, systems).
- Professionals involved in any stage of the software development lifecycle, whether working solo or in large cross-functional teams.
- Artificial Intelligence Agents and Language Models, which act as direct readers of the documentation to develop code with greater precision.
- Product Managers, Software Engineers, Architects, and QA (Quality) teams, who use the generated documents to align requirements and tests across all project phases.

## 3. When? (Quando)
> What is the urgency, seasonality, or time trigger for the use/launch of this system?
- There is no fixed trigger or time prerequisite. Its use is entirely on-demand, according to the user's needs at any point in the pipeline.
- It can be triggered both in the initial planning of a new project, or in the middle of the development cycle to unblock scope and clarity issues.

## 4. Where? (Onde)
> Where will this be accessed and executed? (Platform, Cloud, Local, Web, Mobile).
- The execution environment is completely flexible and agnostic, left to the user's discretion.
- It can run on any platform (such as IDEs, CLI terminals, or Web) as long as the chosen ecosystem supports the native interpretation and use of Skills.

## 5. Why? (Por Quê)
> What is the final success metric? Why are we building this?
- The success of the Skill is evaluated qualitatively and focused on the individual's experience: if the tool generated knowledge gain in the project or induced the user to think further to discover new points, the objective was achieved.
- In summary, the primary success metric is having added any practical or understanding value for those who use it.

## 6. How? (Como)
> What will be the "happy path" or the main execution mechanics for the user?
- The execution flow works like an interview in a fluid, non-rigid chat format.
- The main mechanic is extreme flexibility: the user advances discovering points, with total autonomy to revisit previous questions, adjust already generated documents in real-time, and then resume the flow exactly from where they left off.
