# Compliance and Legal Aspects (12.01_legal_compliance.md)

**Purpose:** Map and formalize the legal risks, data processing (LGPD/GDPR), and possible bureaucratic rules associated with the application's flow. Note: This artifact does not replace professional legal advice.

---

## 1. Privacy Opinion (LGPD/GDPR)
> How does the tool handle user data and what is the risk of a leak?
- The architected **Skill** operates strictly as a set of rules (Prompts and scripts). It does **not** have its own cloud servers, does not capture proprietary telemetry, and does not retain databases.
- **Liability Disclaimer:** The tool runs "on top" of the model and interface (IDE) chosen by the user. During the interview cycles (e.g., `/interview docs` or `/interview resume`), the data read from the local file system is packaged and trafficked directly to the LLM provider's servers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, etc.). LGPD/GDPR compliance, secrecy, and secure processing of this data fall solely and **exclusively** on the AI provider's Terms of Service. The author/creator of the Skill fully exempts themselves from civil liability for leaks or misuse of data trafficked by the third-party API.
- **Critical Mitigation Warning:** It is expressly contraindicated and forbidden for the User to execute this Skill on physical files containing Sensitive Personal Data (PII), database passwords, trade secrets, or raw (hardcoded) API keys. The AI consumes the full scope of the file; injecting keys in this context is a strict operational risk of the operator.

## 2. Contractual and Fiscal Aspects
> Are there bureaucratic, financial rules, or usage limitations attached to the flow?
- **Open Licensing ("AS IS" Clause):** The project is characterized as a freely distributed Open-Source tool. The Skill is delivered to users under the legal premise "AS IS" (with no implied warranties). The original author exempts themselves from the contractual obligation to provide continuous technical support or guarantee the absence of operational bugs with partner APIs.
- **Absolute Financial Exemption:** The Skill's architecture does not process payment methods, has no fiat gateway, and requires no proprietary monthly fee. The generated financial consumption (token billing) is a strict contract between the User and the selected LLM provider. Given this, e-commerce audits, chargeback laws, and transaction taxation are summarily classified as **N/A** (Not Applicable).

## 3. Legal Fallback (Audit Decision)
> Does the designed architecture present any immediate risk that requires review or blocking?
- **Official Auditor Decision:** **[ X ] APPROVED**
- **Justification:** The decentralized architecture of the system was classified as having Very Low Legal Risk because it does not retain files locally on its own servers and has no E-commerce ties. The project receives the official "Green Light" for deployment.
- **Mitigation Caveat (Terms of Use):** Despite the unrestricted structural approval, a preventive communication condition was imposed. A strict block of "Terms of Use" and Disclaimer must be ostensibly posted inside the master `README.md` of the repository. The objective of this clause is to legally protect the author and publicly notify all developers about the risks of leaking before they clone and execute the Skill on sensitive files.
